The storm arrives before you, its thunderous roar shaking the leaves of the ancient trees. The air grows thick with humidity, and the sky darkens to an unsettling gray. A low, ominous hum fills the silence, and the ground trembles as if the earth itself is listening. You hear distant whispers—voices that seem to come from nowhere and everywhere at once. The path ahead becomes treacherous, and the only sound is the steady beat of your own heart against the storm’s fury.
