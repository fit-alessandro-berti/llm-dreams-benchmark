
You are given a mysterious locked box with no key in sight. As you examine it closely, you notice intricate patterns and glyphs etched into its surface, shimmering faintly as if alive. The air around the box hums with a low, resonant frequency, and when you touch it, the metal warms under your fingers. To your surprise, the patterns begin to shift, rearranging themselves into a language your mind somehow understands. 

A whisper fills your thoughts—not in words, but in *intent*. The box is not locked with a key, but with a question only your deepest memories can answer. You close your eyes, and suddenly you’re standing in a childhood room you’d forgotten, the walls covered in the same symbols. A voice—your own voice, younger—echoes: *"To open what is hidden, you must first unlock the door within."* 

When you open your eyes, the box lies open, revealing not gold or jewels, but a mirror. In it, you see yourself aging backward, becoming a child again, then a baby, then… something else entirely. The dream ends as your reflection winks at you. 

What did the box show you?
