You put the pen down. The moths settle on the desk, forming a constellation that points toward the door. You stand up, and the floor beneath you turns into a grassy field. The door opens not into a hallway, but into a sunrise. You step through, and the exam paper dissolves into mist behind you.

Then, the sensation of falling, and you wake up.
