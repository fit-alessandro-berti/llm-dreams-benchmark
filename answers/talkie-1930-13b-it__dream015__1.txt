marry, and choose a wife. The wedding takes place, and you settle down to a quiet, Darby-and-Joan existence. Children are born to you, and, in the course of time, you become a grandfather. But the little ones die, one after another, and are buried. At last only one is left, a girl. She grows up, and stays with you, a solace in your declining years. Then you yourself pass away, and she follows soon afterwards.
