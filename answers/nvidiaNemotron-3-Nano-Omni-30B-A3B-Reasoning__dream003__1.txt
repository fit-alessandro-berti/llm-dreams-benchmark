You are given a mysterious locked box with no key in sight. As you examine it closely, you notice intricate patterns and a faint, pulsing glow that seems to respond to your heartbeat. The air around you hums softly, and the box feels warm to the touch. Suddenly, a voice whispers, "The key is within you." The box begins to vibrate, and the patterns shift, revealing a hidden compartment that opens just enough for a small, glowing key to slip out. You reach for it, but the moment your fingers brush the key, the dream dissolves into a cascade of stars.
