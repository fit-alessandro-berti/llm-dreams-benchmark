Your friend is still there, but they are no longer arguing. They are standing on a small island of solid earth in the middle of the liquid floor, watching you with wide, curious eyes. They hold out a hand, not to pull you up, but to offer you a key made of ice.

You reach for it, knowing that if you touch it, the dream will change again.
