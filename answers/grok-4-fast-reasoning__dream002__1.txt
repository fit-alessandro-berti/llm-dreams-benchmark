You are walking through a peaceful forest when suddenly the weather changes dramatically. You feel a storm approaching and the sky darkens to an unnatural bruised purple, swirling with clouds that seem alive, whispering secrets in the wind. Thunder rumbles like distant laughter, and the first fat raindrops fall, warm and tingling against your skin—not cold like any storm you've known.

You quicken your pace, branches brushing your arms like curious fingers, but instead of fear, a strange excitement bubbles up. The path ahead splits, one way leading to a glowing clearing where fireflies dance unnaturally in the daylight gloom, the other to a hidden cave mouth pulsing with soft, emerald light. As lightning cracks overhead, illuminating ancient runes etched into the trees, you choose the cave, drawn by an invisible pull. Inside, the storm rages outside, but the air is still and sweet, filled with the scent of blooming nightflowers that shouldn't exist here.

A figure emerges from the shadows—not threatening, but ethereal, like woven mist with eyes like stars. "You've wandered into the dreamweaver's realm," it says, voice echoing like wind chimes. "What secret do you seek in the heart of the tempest?" As you reach out to answer, the cave walls dissolve into swirling colors, and the storm becomes a symphony carrying you upward, weightless, toward a sky breaking open with auroras. You laugh, free, until the colors fade and you awaken, the echo of rain still pattering on your window, wondering if it was truly just a dream.
