The boundaries between you and the canvas blur, and for a moment, you feel as though you could step inside the colors and become part of the dream yourself.

Suddenly, the world around you begins to mirror the painting—gravity shifts, the ground beneath your feet becomes soft and shifting like watercolor, and the sky above swirls in a thousand shades of impossible color. You realize you are both the artist and the creation, caught in a loop of endless transformation, where every stroke you make changes not just the canvas, but the very reality you stand within.

And just as you reach out to add another mark, you wake up, the colors fading but the memory of their motion lingering behind your eyes.
