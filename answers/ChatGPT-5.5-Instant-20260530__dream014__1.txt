The strangers smile and gesture for you to follow. Somehow, despite never having met them, they feel familiar. As you walk together, the city begins to transform. Narrow alleys unfold into bright gardens. Concrete sidewalks turn into winding paths lined with glowing lanterns. The noise of traffic fades into distant music.

Each stranger helps in a different way. One carries a map that redraws itself whenever you look away. Another points out symbols hidden throughout the city—small clues you had been missing all along. A third simply listens as you talk about why you feel lost.

Eventually, they lead you to a tall clock tower standing at the center of the city. Its hands move backward and forward at the same time. At the top of the tower is a single door with your name engraved on it.

When you open the door, you expect to find answers. Instead, you find a room filled with scenes from your own life—moments of success, mistakes, friendships, adventures, and opportunities not yet taken. The strangers explain that the city was never a place at all. It was a map of your thoughts, fears, and hopes.

"You weren't lost," one of them says. "You were searching."

As the clock tower begins to glow, the strangers gradually fade away. Before they disappear completely, they leave you with the map. This time, it no longer changes. At its center is a simple message:

**Trust yourself. You already know the way.**

The tower bell rings once, and the entire city lights up like a constellation. Just as you take your first confident step forward, you wake up with the feeling that, somehow, you have remembered something important.
