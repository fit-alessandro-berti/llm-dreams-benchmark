I weave through the crowd, scanning familiar faces in a sea of strangers. A cluster of artists debates color theory near the window; I pause, but none are who I’m looking for. Further on, a circle of musicians laughs over shared stories—still no sign. The air hums with conversation and clinking glasses, yet my friend remains elusive. Just as doubt creeps in, I spot a flash of their signature blue scarf by the bookshelf—relief washes over me like sunlight breaking through clouds.
