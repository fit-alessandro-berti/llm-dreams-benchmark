You are in a world where colors and shapes are constantly changing. As you explore this surreal landscape, you come across a blank canvas and a floating brush that hums with potential. The moment you grasp it, the canvas comes alive: each stroke you make blooms into living forms—ribbons of crimson fire twisting into sapphire fish that swim through air made of melting emerald clocks. Mountains stretch into soft violet waves; trees unfurl as kaleidoscopic fractals that fold and refold themselves. The more you paint, the more the world feeds back into you, until brush, canvas, sky, and self dissolve into one infinite, shimmering current. You ride it for what feels like forever… then awaken gently, the last colors still glowing behind your eyelids, a quiet invitation to create something strange and new today.
