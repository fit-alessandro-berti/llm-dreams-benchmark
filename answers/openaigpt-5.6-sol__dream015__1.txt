You find yourself in a post-apocalyptic world, with resources scarce and dangers everywhere. Despite the hardships, you decide to search for the rumored Garden—a hidden valley where clean water still runs and seeds still grow.

You travel through cities hollowed by wind, their towers leaning like tired giants. At night, pale creatures move beyond the firelight, whispering in voices that sound almost human. Along the way, you rescue a wounded fox with one silver eye. It begins following you, always stopping just before danger appears.

After many days, you reach a wall of black mountains. There, beneath a rusted satellite dish, you discover a sealed door. Your last battery opens it.

Inside is not a garden, but a vast underground chamber filled with sleeping people. Thousands of glass pods glow softly in the dark. A message flickers across a nearby screen:

**THE WORLD ABOVE IS READY. WAKE THEM.**

You hesitate. Outside, thunder rolls beneath a cloudless sky. The fox sits beside the control panel, its silver eye shining with the same blue light as the pods.

Then you understand: the wasteland was never your prison. It was your test.

You press the button.

Across the chamber, lights awaken one by one, until they resemble stars—and for the first time since the end of the world, you hear morning birds singing somewhere above.
