You are in a world where colors and shapes are constantly changing. As you explore this surreal landscape, you come across a blank canvas and **feel an inexplicable pull toward it**, as if it’s waiting for you to bring it to life.

When you touch the canvas, it ripples like water, and suddenly, **your thoughts begin to paint themselves**. A swirl of crimson blooms into a heart, jagged lines of gold burst into a lightning storm, and soft pastels drift in like clouds. The world around you shifts with every stroke — mountains melt into rivers, trees sprout wings, and the sky becomes a mosaic of forgotten memories.

You realize this canvas is not just a surface — it’s a **mirror of your soul**, and every color you choose reveals a hidden part of yourself. As you paint, the landscape responds, growing wilder and more vibrant with each emotion you express.

Suddenly, a figure steps out from behind a shifting wall of green — it’s you, but older, wiser, and smiling. It hands you a brush made of stardust.

**“Now it’s your turn to dream,”** it says.

And with that, the world begins to change again — not just around you, but within you.
