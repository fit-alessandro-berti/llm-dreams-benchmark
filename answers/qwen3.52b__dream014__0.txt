You take one step, and the silence behind your ear becomes a song. 
The crowd moves like a river that doesn't know you're trying to cross it. You stop at a wall made of glass that feels warm against your chest.
When they speak again, their voices echo with a rhythm that matches yours: "You don't have to find your way through them; just walk into the space between the buildings."
And you find that in that quiet gap, you are not lost. You're found. You breathe, and for the first time, you feel entirely whole.
