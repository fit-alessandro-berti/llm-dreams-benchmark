It is a quiet, golden light, where the pattern on the metal feels like a whisper before it breaks. Inside, there is a shadow of a key waiting to be placed inside. The cold air against your skin feels the scent of ozone, just as I remember that moment when we were both trying to find our keys in a strange box with no key in sight. We look at the intricate patterns, the rusted edges feeling like memories that are still held in the glass frame.

The lock turns into the shape of a simple question on paper: *What is the meaning of the pattern?* The answer is not in the code. It is in what we do when we open the box. If you are to understand this dream, you have to imagine the moment before you find it. Is it the key? Or is it the silence that followed us finding it? I can't tell you exactly how to unplug the lock because there's no signal from inside, only the feeling of a locked door opening when the pattern fades.
