you hasten to seek shelter. As you do so, a great oak tree opens, and you walk into a spacious cavern, which is lit by natural lamps. The walls of the cavern are hung with stalactites, and the floor covered with soft carpet. You feel perfectly secure from the storm, which Rages outside, and sit down to rest. After a time you fall asleep. In your sleep you dream that fairies enter and dance before you, and wake singing.
