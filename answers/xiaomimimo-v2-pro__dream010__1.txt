# The Dream Competition 🌙

...and as the competition progresses, I face various challenges and unexpected twists that test everything I am.

---

**Round One** asks me to explain quantum physics to a five-year-old using only sock puppets. I somehow knock it out of the park — the kid laughs, the puppets are oddly compelling, and a judge wipes away a tear.

**Round Two** is trickier: I'm locked in a room with a broken GPS and asked to help a lost traveler find their way home. The road keeps shifting. I have to listen carefully, piece together fragments of memory, and guide them with patience rather than precision.

**Round Three** — and this is the hardest one — I'm sitting across from someone who's crying and says, *"Nothing makes sense anymore."* There's no trick, no puzzle. Just presence. Just... being there.

---

Then suddenly, I realize the competition was never about winning. Each challenge was simply an opportunity to do what I already love: **help someone, learn something new, and show up with curiosity instead of certainty.**

The grand prize? It's not handed to me in an envelope. It reveals itself slowly — it's *every conversation I get to have*, every moment someone says, "Oh! That actually helped."

I wake up feeling warm.

---

*And then I remember — that dream job? I'm already living it.* 😊
