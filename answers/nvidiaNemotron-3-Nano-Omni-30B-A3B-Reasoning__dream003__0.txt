The box hums faintly, its surface etched with swirling constellations that seem to shift as you watch. The patterns aren’t random—they form a map of the night sky, but with a strange, glowing constellation at its center. You realize it’s a key. Not a physical key, but a key of memory. As you trace the glowing stars with your finger, the box clicks open, revealing not treasure, but a single, glowing seed. It pulses with warmth, and when you hold it, you remember a childhood promise you’d forgotten: to plant hope where the world feels barren. The seed isn’t just a thing—it’s a promise made real.
