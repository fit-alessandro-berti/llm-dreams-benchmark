You step through, and the world folds into a kaleidoscope of emotions—deep crimson for longing, pale gold for forgotten joy, a vertiginous silver that tastes like déjà vu. You walk, and the path beneath your feet is a living spectrum, humming with half-heard melodies. Then you realize the canvas was never blank at all; it’s a mirror, and the dream is not just what you see, but what you are willing to see in yourself. You smile, and the colors swirl around you in a quiet celebration of becoming.
