You run your fingers along the seams and feel a faint warmth. There is no obvious lock, only a tiny indentation shaped like a crescent moon. When you press it, the room around you ripples like water.

Suddenly, the box opens not with a click, but with a sigh.

Inside is not treasure, but a folded map of somewhere you’ve never been and yet somehow remember. The ink is silver, and the paths on it rearrange themselves to match your thoughts. As you watch, a single star rises from the paper and floats above your palm.

It pulses once, then twice, and the dream gently says: *follow me.*

You step forward, and the floor becomes a sky.
