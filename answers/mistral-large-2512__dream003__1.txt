**The Dream Continues…**

The patterns on the box shift beneath your fingertips—swirling like ink in water, forming shapes that seem to whisper secrets. One moment, they resemble constellations; the next, they twist into ancient runes or the veins of a living thing. You press your palm against the surface, and the metal (or is it wood? It feels like both) hums faintly, as if responding to your touch.

Then, the box *clicks*.

Not a lock turning—no, it’s something deeper, like a mechanism awakening. The patterns flare with a soft, golden light, and a seam appears where none was before. The lid lifts just a hair’s breadth, revealing a sliver of darkness inside. A scent drifts out: rain on hot stone, or the first breath of a storm. Your pulse quickens.

From the gap, a voice—no, not a voice, but a *feeling*—spills into your mind:

*"You’ve been chosen. But do you dare to see?"*

The box trembles. The light pulses, and the patterns rearrange themselves into a single, unmistakable symbol: a keyhole, perfectly shaped for a key you don’t have. Yet as you stare at it, the symbol *changes*, morphing into something else—a door, a mouth, a wound in the air.

And then, from the darkness inside the box, something *moves*.

A shadow stretches toward you, not threatening, but *curious*. It brushes your wrist, and suddenly you’re no longer standing in the room where you found the box. You’re somewhere else—somewhere vast and echoing, where the walls are made of shifting memories and the floor is a mosaic of forgotten faces. The box is still in your hands, but now it’s *open*, and the thing inside is watching you.

Waiting.

What do you do?
