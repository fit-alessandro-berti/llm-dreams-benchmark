I open my mouth to apologize, to tell them I’ve forgotten my speech, but no human words come out. Instead, a low, resonant musical chord hums from deep within my chest. It fills the silence, carrying the warmth of gold and the scent of ozone. 

The crowd doesn't look confused. They smile, leaning into the sound, and when they breathe out, their collective breath forms a shimmering, aurora-like ribbon that loops around the stage.
