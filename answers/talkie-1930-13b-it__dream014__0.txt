At first you are somewhat embarrassed, but soon feel perfectly at home. In the course of conversation, you discover that the inmates of the house are all ardent lovers of music; and you yourself sing to them. At length they conduct you to a bedroom, where you pass the night, and awake in the morning refreshed and cheerful.
