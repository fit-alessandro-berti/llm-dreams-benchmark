...written in a language I’ve never seen before, yet somehow I understand every word. The first question reads: *“What is the sound of one hand clapping if the hand is made of starlight?”* I blink, and the paper ripples like water. The second question: *“If you could un-invent one thing to save a memory, what would it be and why?”* My pen hovers, but instead of ink, it drips tiny galaxies. The proctor, a tall figure with a face like a shifting kaleidoscope, leans in and whispers, “Don’t answer with your mind. Answer with the part of you that still believes in magic.” I look down and realize the exam isn’t on paper at all—it’s a mirror, and the questions are my own reflection asking them back.
