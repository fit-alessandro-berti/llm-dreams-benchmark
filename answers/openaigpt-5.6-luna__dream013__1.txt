You are in a maze filled with complex puzzles. Each puzzle requires intense concentration and logical thinking to solve, and as you move deeper into the maze, you begin to realize that the walls are shifting behind you, erasing every path you have already taken.

At the center of each chamber is a glowing key, but none of them fit the locks on the doors. Instead, every solution reveals a fragment of a forgotten memory: a childhood bedroom, a voice calling your name, rain tapping against a window. The deeper you go, the more familiar the maze becomes.

At last, you reach a vast room with no walls and no ceiling. In its center stands a mirror. You look into it and see yourself sitting in bed, asleep, dreaming of the maze. Behind your reflection, a final door slowly opens.

You step toward it—and wake up holding a small silver key in your hand.
