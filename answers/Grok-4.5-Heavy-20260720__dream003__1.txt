You are given a mysterious locked box with no key in sight. As you examine it closely, you notice intricate patterns and living glyphs that shift and swirl like ink beneath your fingertips. They rearrange into the constellations of a half-forgotten childhood sky, then into the unique shape of your own eye. When you lean closer, the wood softens and the box unfolds like a blooming flower, revealing not a treasure but a doorway into a corridor of mirrors. Each reflection shows a different version of yourself holding an identical box, and deep within the infinite reflections you hear the soft click of a lock that was never truly locked at all.
