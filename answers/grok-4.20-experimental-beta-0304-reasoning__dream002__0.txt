...and the sky cracks open like an eggshell.

The peaceful forest you were strolling through moments ago now feels alive in a different way—trees bending and whispering as the wind whips through their leaves. Rain begins to fall, but not ordinary rain. Each drop glows faintly, like liquid starlight, and when they touch your skin, they carry fragments of forgotten memories that aren't quite yours.

Thunder rolls, deep and resonant, but instead of fear, you feel a strange exhilaration. The path ahead splits. One way remains the quiet forest trail, now darkening under the storm. The other... the trees part to reveal a glowing door standing alone in the middle of the woods, framed by twisting vines and pulsing with soft violet light.

Lightning flashes, and in that split second, you see *something* moving between the trees—tall, elegant silhouettes that definitely aren't deer. They watch you with luminous eyes.

You feel the dream tugging at you, offering a choice:

Do you run toward the glowing door, or follow the strange figures deeper into the storm?

The rain is getting heavier now, and with every drop, the world feels a little less solid... like it's waiting for you to decide what happens next.
