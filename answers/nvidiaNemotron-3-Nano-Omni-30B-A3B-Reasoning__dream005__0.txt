You are at a large social gathering where you only know one person. As you try to find them, you encounter various groups of people and find yourself in a surreal conversation with a talking cat who insists that the party is actually a cosmic dance floor where the stars are spinning in rhythm with your heartbeat. The cat, wearing a tiny tuxedo and a monocle, offers to guide you through the crowd, leading you past tables of laughing strangers, a fountain that sings operatic arias, and a group of musicians playing instruments made of glass. As you follow, the cat points out your one acquaintance, now perched on a floating lantern, waving a glowing map that seems to rewrite itself with each step you take. The cat whispers that the only way to truly find your friend is to dance, and as you move to the music of the stars, the entire gathering dissolves into a cascade of shimmering constellations that swirl around you, revealing that the party was never a gathering at all, but a dream‑like rehearsal for a journey beyond the night sky.
