The argument isn't gone, but it's become... a texture on the page, a dark ink blot that slowly transforms into the contour of a shared horizon. I look up. My friend is still there, but now they’re smiling gently, not in triumph, but in quiet recognition. The storm in the room hasn’t vanished—it’s settled into the soil, and something small and green is pushing up between us.
