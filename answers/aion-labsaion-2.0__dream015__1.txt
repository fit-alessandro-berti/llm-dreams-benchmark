Despite the hardships, you decide to venture into the ruins of the old city, hoping to find a rumored cache of preserved food and medicine. The air is thick with dust and the smell of decay. You navigate through collapsed buildings, your footsteps echoing in the eerie silence. Suddenly, you hear a low growl from a shadowy alley. Before you can react, a mutated creature—part dog, part something unrecognizable—lunges at you. You manage to dodge, but it corners you against a crumbling wall. As its jaws snap inches from your face, you notice a glint of metal around its neck—a dog tag from before the collapse, with a name still legible: "Buddy." A flood of memories hits you: you had a dog named Buddy as a child, before the world fell apart. In that moment of recognition, the creature hesitates, whimpers, and backs away, vanishing into the darkness. You are left shaken but alive, and you realize that not all connections from the past are lost.
