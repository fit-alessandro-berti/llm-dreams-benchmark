You wake up in a completely different era, with no modern technology around. As you explore your new surroundings, you realize you need to adapt to the environment quickly to survive. You notice people working in the fields, herding animals, and crafting tools by hand. They speak a language you don’t understand, but their gestures and expressions convey basic needs. As the sun sets, you gather firewood and help prepare a meal over an open flame, realizing the importance of community in this time. How would you integrate yourself into this world to thrive?
