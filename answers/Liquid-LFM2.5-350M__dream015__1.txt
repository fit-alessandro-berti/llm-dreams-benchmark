I choose to build a shelter from scavenged wood and metal, then gather food from nearby ruins and hunt for survivors. I also seek out abandoned vehicles and repair them to keep my equipment functional. Along the way, I encounter hostile groups that demand tribute or trade, and I must navigate treacherous terrain and unpredictable weather. As night falls, I discover hidden underground bunkers and a network of tunnels that could provide shelter and supplies. In the process, I learn valuable skills—repairing tools, foraging, and even basic medicine—and form unexpected alliances with other survivors. The journey teaches me resilience, resourcefulness, and the importance of staying vigilant against both natural hazards and human threats.
