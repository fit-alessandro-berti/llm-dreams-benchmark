For a time all goes well; but cares increase, and you begin to perceive that you must work harder if you would live. Still, you are happy in your little home, until ill-health comes to you, and you can no longer pursue your avocation. Then darker days ensue. Misfortune follows misfortune; the home has to be given up; friends grow cool, and enemies crowd around you. In despair, you enlist for a soldier, and, going abroad, fight in foreign lands. At length, peace is restored, and you return to find yourself forgotten by all, save by the companion of your early years, who remains faithful to the last. At last you die, and she follows quickly after.
