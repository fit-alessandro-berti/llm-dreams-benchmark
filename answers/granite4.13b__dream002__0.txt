You kneel down beside it and gaze up at the illuminated symbols above; they shift slightly with each passing second, as if alive to your presence.

The rain patters steadily outside, but inside the forest, there’s a sense of harmony between chaos and tranquility, guiding your thoughts toward introspection and wonder.

After some time, the storm gradually eases its intensity, lightening clouds revealing patches of sun. Walking away from the altar, you carry with you not just memories of this experience but also a renewed appreciation for nature’s power to both humble and inspire us in equal measure.
