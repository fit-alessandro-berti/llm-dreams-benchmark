The first question asks: **“Why did you leave the room last time?”**

You look up. The classroom is empty except for the proctor, who has your face but older eyes. Outside the windows, the sky is the color of erased pencil marks.

You turn the page.

Every question describes something you have not done yet.

**“At what age will you stop answering the phone?”**  
**“Name the person whose apology you will never hear.”**  
**“Show your work.”**

Your pencil begins moving before you touch it.
