You realize then that in this dream, the apocalypse was just a blank canvas, and the scarcity was a test of imagination. The resources weren't water or fuel; they were memories, hopes, and the sheer force of will you carry within you.

A path of floating stepping-stones extends toward a massive, dormant engine at the center of the library. It's ticking with a rhythm that matches your own heartbeat. You understand now: you decide to **wind the key in the engine's chest, not to restart the old world, but to dream it anew, with all the beauty you remember and none of the fear.**

As you turn the key, the glass floor beneath you shatters into butterflies, and the dream begins to shift according to your heart's desire. What do you paint next?
