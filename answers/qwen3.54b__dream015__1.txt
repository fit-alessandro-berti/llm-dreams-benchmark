Despite the hardships, you decide to **plant a garden from your own lost memories.** The seeds you hold are warm, and when they fall onto the concrete, they don't rot; instead, they bloom into tiny, glowing stars that float upward like smoke. You realize that the monsters in the wasteland aren't attacking you at all—they are simply trying to tell you a story written in riddles only your heart can read. As the dawn approaches, you see yourself not as a survivor, but as a seed waiting to be watered by a rain that never ends, and in the soft light of the waking world, you smile because it was finally time to grow again.
