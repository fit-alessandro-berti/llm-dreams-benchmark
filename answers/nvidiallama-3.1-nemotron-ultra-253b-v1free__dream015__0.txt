Your first lead takes you to a ramshackle settlement on the outskirts of the ruins, a place called New Haven. It's a lawless sprawl of makeshift shelters and scavenged goods, where information is the most valuable currency. You have a name to find there—Raven, a mysterious trader with ties to The Architect. But be warned, Raven is not one to be trusted lightly. Their allegiances are as shifting as the sands that now cover the old highways.

Upon entering New Haven, you're immediately struck by the cacophony of sounds and the desperate eyes of its inhabitants. You make your way through the crowded market, where everything from bottled water to jury-rigged weapons is on display.
