**You are dreaming. Can you complete the following dream?**

You are given a mysterious locked box with no key in sight. As you examine it closely, you notice intricate patterns...  

---  
The patterns shift as you stare, like liquid silver tracing constellations across its surface. They’re not just symbols—they seem to *breathe*, pulsing faintly in time with a heartbeat you don’t remember having. When you reach out a trembling finger, the metal grows warm. A whisper rises, not from your ears but your *bones*: *"To open, remember what you’ve forgotten."*  

The box tilts slightly, revealing a hidden seam. You pry it open—and instead of a keyhole, there’s a miniature mirror, cracked down the middle. In its shards, you see flashes of a childhood bedroom, a lighthouse, and a door you’ve never seen before. The air thickens. The box’s patterns now spell a single word in your own handwriting: **"NOW."**  

You press your palm to the mirror. It cracks further, and the room dissolves into light. The box clicks shut behind you, heavier now, as if it’s been waiting for you to *become* the key.  

---  
*(The dream ends, but the box remains on your nightstand. It hums.)*
