You are the captain of a ship in the middle of a turbulent sea. Your crew looks to you for guidance as the storm worsens, and you **feel the timbers of the ship groan as a shadowy figure materializes on the deck—a woman in a tattered captain’s coat, her face obscured by mist. She points toward a jagged island shrouded in thunderclouds ahead, where a crumbling lighthouse pulses with an eerie green light.**  

**You bark orders to batten down the hatches, but your crew hesitates. The woman’s voice booms over the wind: *"Trust the dark, or be swallowed by it."***  

**Do you steer toward the island’s treacherous waters, risking the unknown, or fight against the current, knowing the storm might tear the ship apart? The sea seems to answer before you decide—waves part slightly, revealing a path of bioluminescent plankton glowing like stars beneath the waves.**  

**But as the lighthouse light flickers, you realize the woman is smiling. The crew, now silent, begins chanting in a language no one should know. The ship tilts, not into the storm, but *into* the mist itself. The dream fractures into a thousand voices, and you wake to the sound of dripping water… in a cave.**  

What do you do next?
