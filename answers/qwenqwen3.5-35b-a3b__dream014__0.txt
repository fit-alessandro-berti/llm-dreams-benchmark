You are feeling lost and alone in a bustling city. Suddenly, a group of kind strangers offers to help you find your way, and you **accept their guidance without a second thought, as if you have known them your entire life.**

The streets beneath your feet transform from cold concrete into a mossy, springy path that glows with a gentle, bioluminescent blue.

You aren't walking toward a destination anymore; you are walking toward a feeling of home that you didn't know you had lost. The strangers laugh softly, their voices blending with the wind in a way that sounds like a secret you are remembering. One of them offers you a hand that feels like warm sunlight, and as you take it, you realize you aren't being led to a specific address at all.

They lead you to a door that appears in the middle of a park, and when you open it, you see a room full of light. Inside, there are no walls, only an endless horizon of calm. You sit down on a cushion that floats just above the ground, and the strangers fade into the light, leaving you there, not because you have arrived, but because you have finally found where you were meant to be.

And then, the light expands to fill your vision, and you wake up on your bed, the morning sun hitting your face, carrying the lingering warmth of the dream in your chest, leaving you feeling more rested than you have in years.
