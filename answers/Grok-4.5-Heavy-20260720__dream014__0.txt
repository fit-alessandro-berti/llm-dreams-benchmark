You are feeling lost and alone in a bustling city. Suddenly, a group of kind strangers offers to help you find your way, and you take their outstretched hands. Instantly the roar of the city softens into a gentle hum. Neon lights swirl like fireflies, the pavement softens to moss underfoot, and the strangers’ faces glow with a soft, familiar light—reflections of every kindness and courage you’ve ever known. They guide you through a narrow alley that blooms into a luminous floating garden of whispering trees and lanterns high above the skyline. At its heart stands a quiet wooden door that opens onto a sunlit place that feels exactly like home. You step through, the loneliness dissolving completely, and wake with a deep sense of belonging still lingering in your chest, knowing you were never truly alone.
