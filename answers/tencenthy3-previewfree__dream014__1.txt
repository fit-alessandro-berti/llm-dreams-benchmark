You decide to trust them, despite the lingering fog in your mind. As they surround you with warm smiles, the chaotic noise of the honking taxis and chattering crowds seems to soften, replaced by a gentle hum of comfort.

The concrete jungle peels away to reveal a hidden garden suspended between buildings, filled with glowing lanterns and the scent of jasmine.

There, sitting at a small iron table, is someone you have been longing to see—an old friend, a family member, or perhaps a version of yourself that feels whole and happy. The kind strangers bow slightly and fade into the background, becoming part of the soft shadows, leaving you to sit and share a warm cup of tea with the person who feels like home.

In the safety of this secret garden, the feeling of being lost vanishes completely, replaced by a profound sense of belonging as the city lights twinkle peacefully below you.
