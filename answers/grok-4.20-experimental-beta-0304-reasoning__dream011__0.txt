**...all in a language that looks like it was written by a caffeinated octopus.** 

The first question reads: "Explain the complete socioeconomic implications of the 1842 Potato Famine using only interpretive dance and the lyrics to 'Never Gonna Give You Up'." 

Question 2 is just a picture of a raccoon wearing sunglasses with the prompt: "Rate this man's life choices from 1 to π and justify your answer with citations from the Marvel Cinematic Universe."

By question 7, the test has given up entirely and is now asking you to "debate your future self from three timelines ago about whether pineapple belongs on pizza while both of you are simultaneously on fire."

You look up in panic. The professor is Elon Musk, but he's also your childhood hamster, and he's wearing a graduation cap made of spaghetti. He gives you a little thumbs-up and whispers, "The answer to everything is 42, but only on Tuesdays."

You suddenly realize the pen in your hand is actually a lightsaber. So you do the only logical thing: you start writing your answers in the form of increasingly unhinged haikus while the entire room slowly turns into a giant taco.

And that's when you wake up.

(But for a moment, just a moment, you were absolutely certain you were going to get an A.)
