You are walking through a peaceful forest when suddenly the weather changes dramatically. You feel a storm approaching and quicken your pace, the once-gentle rustle of leaves now a frantic whisper as dark clouds swallow the sky. Thunder rumbles like a distant beast awakening, and the first fat raindrops splatter on your skin, cool and insistent.

You duck under the canopy of ancient oaks, but the wind howls through the branches, bending them like weary dancers. Lightning cracks the heavens, illuminating hidden paths you've never noticed before—a glowing trail of mushrooms pulsing with ethereal blue light, leading deeper into the woods. Curiosity pulls you forward despite the downpour, your clothes clinging like a second skin.

As you follow the trail, the storm intensifies, but so does the magic: the rain tastes like stardust, and the trees seem to lean in, murmuring secrets in a language of creaks and sighs. Suddenly, the path opens to a hidden glade where the storm swirls in a perfect vortex, revealing a shimmering portal at its center—a doorway to forgotten dreams.

You step closer, heart pounding, and just as your hand reaches out, the world tilts. The thunder fades to an alarm clock's buzz, and you jolt awake in your bed, the echo of rain still pattering against your window. Was it real? The faint scent of wet earth lingers in the air.
