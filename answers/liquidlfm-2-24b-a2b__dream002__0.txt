…the sky darkens in an instant, the air thickening with the scent of ozone and damp earth. The gentle rustle of leaves transforms into a roar as wind whips through the trees, sending branches swaying violently. Rain begins to fall in thick, stinging sheets, blurring your vision and making the forest floor a slippery maze of mud and fallen branches.

You stumble forward, heart pounding, as the storm seems to close in from all sides. The trees loom like ancient sentinels, their shadows dancing wildly in the lightning flashes. Somewhere in the distance, you hear a low rumble—like thunder, or perhaps something else entirely.

Suddenly, a flash of lightning illuminates a clearing ahead. In its center stands a lone figure, cloaked and hooded, holding a lantern that glows with an eerie, golden light. The figure turns, and you realize it’s not human—its eyes shimmer like stars, and its voice echoes in your mind rather than your ears:

**“You have been chosen. The storm was not natural. It was a warning.”**

Before you can respond, the wind howls again, and the world dissolves into white.
