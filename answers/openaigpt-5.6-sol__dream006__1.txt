The crowd leans closer. Each person sees a different message.

At the back of the hall, someone begins to applaud. The sound spreads until the room trembles, and the stage beneath your feet becomes the deck of a ship. Velvet curtains billow into sails. Rows of seats dissolve into dark, glittering waves.

You grip the podium as the ship rises toward a moon hanging impossibly low over the water. On its pale surface is a door, slightly open, with warm light spilling through.

The silver birds fly toward it.

Without knowing why, you follow.
