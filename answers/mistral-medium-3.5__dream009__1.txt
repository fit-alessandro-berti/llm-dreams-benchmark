You wake up in a completely different era, with no modern technology around. As you explore your new surroundings, you realize you need to **adapt to survive**.

The air smells of earth and woodsmoke, and the people you encounter wear simple, handmade garments. They speak in a dialect you barely understand, but their gestures are clear—curiosity, caution, and a hint of suspicion. You notice the sun is your only clock, and the land is both beautiful and untamed.

You realize you need to **learn the ways of this time**—how to find food, shelter, and perhaps even allies. A kind elder offers you a bowl of stew, and as you eat, you wonder: *Is this a test? A gift? Or have you truly stepped into the past?*

What do you do next?
