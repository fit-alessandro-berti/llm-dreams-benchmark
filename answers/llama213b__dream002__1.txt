
Suddenly, dark clouds gather above me, and strong winds start to blow. The trees around me begin to sway and creak, and I can hear the distant rumble of thunder. The forest floor becomes soft and spongy underfoot, and I realize that I am walking through a dense fog. The air is heavy with the scent of damp earth and decaying leaves.

As I continue to walk, the storm intensifies, and I can feel the electricity in the air. The trees around me begin to crackle with power, and I can hear the sound of leaves and branches crashing to the ground. The fog becomes thicker, making it difficult to see more than a few feet in front of me.

Suddenly, a bolt of lightning strikes a tree nearby, illuminating the dark forest in a brilliant flash. The thunderclap that follows shakes the ground beneath my feet, and I feel a jolt of fear run through my body. I quicken my pace, hoping to find shelter before the storm becomes too intense.

As I walk, the sky above me continues to churn with dark clouds and flashes of lightning. The wind howls through the trees, causing branches to whip back and forth like wild animals. I stumble and almost fall, but manage to catch myself just in time.

I see a small clearing up ahead, partially sheltered by a group of tall trees. I make my way towards it, hoping to find some respite from the storm. As I enter the clearing, I feel a sense of peace wash over me, and the intensity of the storm begins to subside. The clouds begin to part, revealing a glimpse of the bright blue sky beyond.
