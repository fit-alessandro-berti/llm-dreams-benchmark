Days turn into weeks as you work side by side with the survivors, nurturing plants and sharing knowledge of nutrition and preservation. The greenhouse begins to flourish, offering fresh produce that revitalizes your group and provides a glimmer of hope for the future.

Meanwhile, news spreads of a distant enclave rumored to possess advanced technology and resources. Drawn by the promise of something greater, you decide to embark on a perilous journey to reach this enclave, knowing it could hold the key to reclaiming what was lost.
