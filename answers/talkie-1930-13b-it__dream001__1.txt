The hour of the meeting draws near, but still you know not where to go. In this perplexity, you cast your eyes upward, and observe a little door in the tower, which you immediately ascend, and are conducted into a spacious gallery, where you find the persons assembled whom you seek. Here all seems right, and the business of the meeting goes forward. At length it is concluded, and you descend. But now a new trouble arises. How are you to get down? The great west door is shut, and you cannot make it heard. In vain you try. The noise of the city precludes all other sounds. At last, however, a little wicket is opened, and you escape, but not without some damage to your clothing.
