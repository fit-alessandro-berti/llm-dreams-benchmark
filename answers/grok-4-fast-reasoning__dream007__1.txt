You are in the middle of a heated argument with a close friend. The disagreement is about something important to both of you, and you decide to challenge them to a duel at dawn—not with swords, but with ukuleles on the edge of a floating island in the sky. As the first notes strum out under a canopy of swirling auroras, the argument melts into laughter, and the island begins to drift toward a horizon where the sun rises in reverse, pulling you both into a dream within the dream where old wounds heal with every chord.
