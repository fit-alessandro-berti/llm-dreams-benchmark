# The Gathering

You are at a large social gathering where you only know one person. As you try to find them, you encounter various groups of people and they all seem to recognize you, though you have no idea who they are.

A woman in a blue dress grabs your arm and says, "You're late! The presentation started without you." She leads you down a hallway that wasn't there a moment ago. The walls are lined with photographs, and in every single one, you are standing just at the edge of the frame, looking away.

You pull free and step into a kitchen. Someone is stirring a enormous pot. "Have you seen—" you start, but they shush you, pointing upward. The ceiling is gone. Above you, there is only an orange sky, and birds flying in perfect geometric formations.

You remember you're looking for someone. You can almost see their face. The name is right there, on the tip of your tongue.

You open a door and find yourself back at the party, but now everyone has stopped talking. They're all watching you. Someone you almost recognize steps forward and asks:

"Were you looking for me?"

And you realize—you aren't sure anymore.

You wake before you can answer.
