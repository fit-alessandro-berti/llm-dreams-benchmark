You are in a world where colors and shapes are constantly changing. As you explore this surreal landscape, you come across a blank canvas and a brush of pure light materializes in your hand. The blank canvas ripples like liquid mercury as the swirling colors and morphing shapes of the landscape pour into it of their own accord, forming fleeting images — a melting clock that transforms into a flock of geometric birds, a spiral staircase dissolving into rainbow smoke. You dip your hand into the surface and pull out a glowing thread of cyan; the sky above instantly rearranges into crystalline lattices. With every touch the boundary softens further until the canvas expands around you. You can no longer tell whether you are painting the dream or the dream is painting you. In that perfect, shifting moment, everything feels beautifully complete.
