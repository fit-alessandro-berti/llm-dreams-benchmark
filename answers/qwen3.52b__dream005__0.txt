You are dreaming. Can you complete the following dream?

As you try to find them, you encounter various groups of people and strangers who begin to hold up mirrors that show only you staring at them until they stop and all face the crowd for real. The one person you know is standing at the edge of the room, watching you like they know something about how you are going to leave. The air grows cold and the crowd begins to dissolve into light.

It turns out you were never alone in the dream.
