You are dreaming. Can you complete the following dream?

You are given a mysterious locked box with no key in sight. As you examine it closely, you notice intricate patterns and **a low, rhythmic hum begins to vibrate within them, syncing with your own heartbeat.** 

The metal feels warm against your fingers—no longer just an object of cold steel, but something that feels like it's been touched before. "This is the key," you whisper as you trace a line around the edge where the patterns shift and change color in time with your pulse. The box remains silent, though. But when you look deep enough into the darkness within its metal structure, you see it isn't locked at all, because **the patterns themselves form the mechanism of the lock.**  

When you touch one, it shifts the others, revealing itself. It was never a box with a key; it was a door waiting to be entered by feeling its weight and seeing its shadow. As you open it, no contents fall out—instead, you feel a sense of clarity filling your hands as you realize that this was always what is being given to you. Now you know... you are dreaming.
