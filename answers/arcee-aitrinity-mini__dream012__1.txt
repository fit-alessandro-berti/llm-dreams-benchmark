3.  **Demonstrated Resolve:** Shows the captain's unwavering focus ("Your gaze is fixed on the treacherous path") and control ("Your hand remains steady").
4.  **Trust & Action:** Highlights the crew's reliance ("Weary but trusting") and their swift execution of orders.
5.  **Visual Progress:** Shows the ship actively responding ("The vessel, groaning and straining, begins to turn") and identifies the goal ("the faint glimmer of safety").
6.  **Unity:** Emphasizes the shared struggle ("We ride this storm out together!").

The dream ends with the captain actively navigating through the immediate crisis, demonstrating competence and fostering the crew's trust in the face of danger.
