You are at your workplace, and suddenly, you are given a project with an impossible deadline. Your boss is waiting for you to start, and you realize that the office walls are melting, the ceiling turning into a swirling galaxy. The deadline is not a date but a riddle: finish before the last star fades. Your boss, now a silhouette of smoke, hands you a quill that writes with light. As you begin, the words float off the page, forming constellations that tell the story of what could be. With each sentence, the room expands, and you understand that the project is to design a new reality. Just as the final word takes shape, a gust of wind sweeps you away, and you awaken, the taste of cosmic ink on your tongue.
