You are walking through a peaceful forest when suddenly the weather changes dramatically. You feel a storm approaching and the once-gentle breeze becomes a powerful wind that bends the trees around you. Dark clouds swirl overhead, moving far too quickly to seem natural.

Instinctively, you search for shelter.

As the first drops of rain begin to fall, you notice a narrow path that wasn't there before. It winds deeper into the forest, glowing faintly with silver light. With thunder rumbling in the distance, you decide to follow it.

The path leads you to an enormous ancient tree. Its trunk is wider than a house, and a doorway has somehow formed in its bark. Another flash of lightning illuminates strange symbols carved around the entrance. Though you feel nervous, something tells you that this is exactly where you are supposed to be.

You step inside.

The storm outside vanishes instantly. The interior of the tree is impossibly large, containing a spiral staircase that stretches upward into darkness. Tiny floating lights drift through the air like curious fireflies.

At the top of the staircase, you discover a room with a single window. Through it, you can see the entire forest—but it has changed. Rivers of light flow between the trees, and countless glowing paths connect places you have visited throughout your life. Some lead to childhood memories, others to moments that have not happened yet.

In the center of the room stands an old clock. Its hands spin wildly before slowing to a stop. Instead of numbers, the clock face shows choices you have made and choices you never took.

As you reach out to touch it, the storm returns—not outside, but within the clock itself. Thunder echoes from inside the glass. Suddenly the hands begin moving again and point toward a path shining brighter than all the others.

A voice whispers, "Every storm is also a doorway."

The clock opens like a gate.

You step through and find yourself standing beneath a clear sky. The forest is calm again. The storm has passed. Ahead lies the glowing path, stretching toward the horizon.

You begin walking, feeling strangely certain that wherever it leads, it is exactly where you need to go.

And just before you discover what waits at the end, you wake up.
