You find yourself in a post-apocalyptic world, with resources scarce and dangers everywhere. Despite the hardships, you decide to gather the scattered survivors you meet and found a hidden sanctuary devoted to preserving what remains of human knowledge.

In the half-collapsed library of an old university, you and your growing band clear the rubble, plant gardens from scavenged seeds, and carefully restore every book, schematic, and data drive you can find. Days are spent fortifying the walls against the roaming threats of the wasteland; nights are spent around the fire teaching children how to read faded manuals, sharing stories of the world that was, and mapping a future built on ingenuity rather than fear. In the dream the yellowed pages begin to glow faintly under the starlight, as if the universe itself is quietly approving. You wake with a quiet, stubborn determination.
