You open one, and the words leap off the page, forming images and scenes that play out around you. Each story you read transports you to different worlds—some filled with adventure, others with peace and tranquility.

In this forest, you meet creatures that communicate through colors and music. They guide you to a clearing where a grand celebration is taking place. Everyone is dancing, and as you join in, you realize that the celebration is for you—a welcome to this ever-changing world.

The dream continues to evolve, with each scene flowing seamlessly into the next. You feel a profound sense of connection and belonging, knowing that in this world, anything is possible, and every moment is an opportunity to create and explore.
