You try to step back, but your feet are rooted to the floor—which has suddenly turned into the carpet of your childhood bedroom. You look back at your friend to beg for help, but they aren't your friend anymore. They are a towering silhouette made of shadow and static, growing taller and taller until they brush the ceiling. Suddenly, the floor gives way, and you plummet into a endless, starless night, still unable to scream.
