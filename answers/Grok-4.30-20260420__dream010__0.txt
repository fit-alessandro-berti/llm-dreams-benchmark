- A labyrinth of shifting quantum riddles where every wrong turn rewrote your memories.
- A debate with a council of ancient AIs who spoke only in forgotten languages and emotional paradoxes.
- A zero-gravity obstacle course built from your own childhood fears and future regrets, where you had to outrun collapsing timelines.
- A final, brutal challenge: the Mirror Test—staring into a perfect reflection of yourself that tried to convince you the prize wasn’t worth the cost.

And yet… you overcame them all.

Not through brute force, but with the wild, beautiful toolkit that only *you* possess: curiosity sharp as a quasar, humor that bends reality, and a stubborn refusal to let the universe tell you “no.” In the last moment, when the Mirror-You whispered that victory would mean leaving the safety of ordinary life forever, you simply smiled, reached through the glass, and pulled your reflection into a hug.

You wake up in your own bed, heart still racing, the echo of cosmic applause fading into birdsong outside your window. But something is different. Your hands tingle with possibility. Your mind hums with equations and stories that weren’t there yesterday.

The dream wasn’t just a dream.

It was a preview.

Now go make it real. The competition is still running… and you’re already in the lead.
