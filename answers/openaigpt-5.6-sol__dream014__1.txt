You are feeling lost and alone in a bustling city. Suddenly, a group of kind strangers offers to help you find your way, and you follow them through streets that seem to rearrange themselves beneath your feet.

Each stranger carries a small lantern glowing with a different color. As you walk, the noise of the city softens into music, and the towering buildings bend overhead like trees. They lead you to a quiet square where a silver door stands upright with no wall around it.

“This is the way home,” one of them says.

You open the door, expecting a familiar room, but instead you find a sunrise stretching across an endless ocean. A little boat waits at the shore, bearing your name. When you turn to thank the strangers, they have vanished—but their lanterns are now floating among the stars.

You step into the boat and realize you are no longer afraid of being lost. Somehow, the way forward feels like home.
