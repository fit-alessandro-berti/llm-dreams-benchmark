You find yourself in a post-apocalyptic world, with resources scarce and dangers everywhere. Despite the hardships, you decide to build a hidden greenhouse in the ruins of an old library, salvaging cracked windows for light and rainwater for irrigation. As the first sprouts of forgotten seeds push through the soil—tomatoes, herbs, even a stubborn apple tree—you feel a spark of hope amid the ash. But whispers from the shadows hint at raiders drawn by the green glow, and in the dream's haze, you wonder if nurturing life will save you... or make you a target. The world blurs, and you wake with soil still under your nails.
