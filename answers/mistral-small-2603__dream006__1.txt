Just as you’re about to bolt off the stage (if only you had legs!), a single spotlight flickers to life, casting a narrow beam over the crowd. *"Well, if you’re going to be a ghost, at least be a *good* one."*

The crowd falls silent again. You stare at her, desperate for any clue. She smiles, adjusts her hat, and adds:

*"Now, tell them about the time you forgot your pants at the grocery store. That’ll wake ‘em up."*
