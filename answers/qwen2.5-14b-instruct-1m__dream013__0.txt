You must navigate through these mental obstacles, facing each fear head-on, until you reach a point where you can finally let go of the past and move forward.

It reflects back all the puzzles you’ve solved, showing how they were interconnected, and how each challenge was a piece of a larger, more profound puzzle—one that has been testing not just your mind, but your spirit and your courage.

With the crystal in hand, you feel a sense of peace wash over you. The walls of the maze begin to dissolve, and you find yourself standing in a vast, open field.
