You are the captain of a ship in the middle of a turbulent sea. Your crew looks to you for guidance as the storm worsens, and you raise your voice above the howling wind, gripping the helm with both hands. "Hold fast!" you shout, your voice cutting through the chaos. "We'll weather this! Trust in the course we've set!" The deck shudders beneath your feet, and the sails snap like thunder. The crew braces, eyes wide with fear and faith, as the ship lurches violently. You scan the horizon—dark clouds churn, but a sliver of moonlight pierces through, steady and bright. "Steady as she goes," you murmur, adjusting the rudder with precision. "We are not lost. We are becoming." The storm rages on, but your resolve burns brighter than any gale.
