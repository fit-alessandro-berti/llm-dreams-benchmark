...**relearn how to listen.**

In the absence of the constant, low-frequency hum of electricity and the distant drone of engines, the silence is heavy and absolute. You realize that to survive this era—to navigate its dense forests and decipher the intentions of the people dressed in rough-spun wool—you can't rely on Google Maps or Wikipedia. You have to read the shift in the wind, the bend of the grass, and the fear in a stranger’s eyes. 

You realize you need to **prove you are useful**, because in a world built on muscle, horse power, and candlelight, there is no room for someone who only knows how to type.
