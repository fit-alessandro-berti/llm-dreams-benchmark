You are given a mysterious locked box with no key in sight. As you examine it closely, you notice intricate patterns and symbols carved into its surface. The designs seem to shift whenever you look away, rearranging themselves into new shapes and unfamiliar alphabets.

Curious, you run your fingers along the carvings. The moment you do, the box emits a soft golden glow. A distant melody echoes through the air, as if coming from somewhere far beyond the horizon. The forest around you falls silent. Even the wind seems to pause.

One symbol suddenly remains still while the others continue moving. It resembles a small doorway. When you press it, the box clicks.

Not open—just one click.

The ground beneath your feet transforms into a giant mosaic matching the patterns on the box. Realizing they are connected, you step onto the tiles that correspond to the symbols glowing on the lid. With each correct step, another click echoes through the dream.

At last, the final lock releases.

The lid slowly opens.

Inside, you do not find treasure, jewels, or ancient artifacts. Instead, you find countless tiny lights floating in darkness. Each light contains a memory: moments from your life, forgotten conversations, childhood adventures, paths not taken, and dreams you once abandoned. They drift around you like stars.

One light shines brighter than the rest.

As you touch it, the surrounding world dissolves. Suddenly, you are standing on a mountaintop beneath a sky filled with unfamiliar constellations. The box now rests open beside you, empty. A voice, calm and distant, whispers:

*"The box was never locked to keep something in. It was locked to keep you from opening it before you were ready."*

The stars begin to descend toward the earth like lanterns. As they touch the ground, new roads appear, stretching in every direction. You realize each road represents a different future.

Just as you take your first step toward one of them, the mountain, the stars, and the mysterious box fade into white light—

—and you wake up with the strange feeling that you have remembered something important, even though you cannot quite recall what it was.
