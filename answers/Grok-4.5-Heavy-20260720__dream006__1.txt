You are about to give a speech to a large audience. As you step onto the stage, you see the crowd looking expectantly at you, and you begin to speak, but instead of words, a cascade of glowing butterflies streams from your mouth, each wing shimmering with a piece of the speech you meant to give. The audience gasps in delight as the butterflies swirl through the hall, alighting on outstretched hands and whispering the message directly into their minds. You smile, realizing no prepared notes could ever match this.
