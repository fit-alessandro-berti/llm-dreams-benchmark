At the end of the path, there is no street, no intersection, and no more crowds. Instead, there is a vast, starlit ocean where the buildings float like paper lanterns on the tide.

One of the strangers reaches out and takes your hand. Their touch is warm, and in that moment, the heavy weight of being lost evaporates.
