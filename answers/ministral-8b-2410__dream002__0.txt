The storm intensifies, and the woman takes your hand, leading you deeper into the forest. "Come," she says, "I need your help. The heart of the forest is in danger." As you follow her, the lantern's light guides your way, pushing back the darkness, and you feel a sense of purpose. The storm rages on, but you're no longer afraid.
