You turn the page. There is no page. Instead, a hallway stretches out where the paper should be, lined with mirrors showing you at different ages, all of them taking the same exam with different expressions of panic, curiosity, and calm. A proctor with no face gently taps your desk and says, “Time is only a suggestion here.”

The next question appears in the air:

**If your future career is a door, what are you that turns the handle?**

You reach for a pencil, but it has become a key. The room waits.
