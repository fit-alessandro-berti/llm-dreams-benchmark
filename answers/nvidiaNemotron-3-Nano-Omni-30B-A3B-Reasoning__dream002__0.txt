The air grows heavy, and the wind begins to whisper through the trees, carrying the scent of rain. The sunlight dims as thick, dark clouds roll in from the horizon, swallowing the golden glow. The peaceful rustling of leaves turns into a frantic dance as branches sway violently. You quicken your pace, but the path ahead seems to twist and shift, as if the forest itself is alive and resisting your passage.

A distant rumble echoes through the trees—thunder, deep and rolling, vibrating in your chest. Rain begins to fall in cold, silver sheets, soaking your clothes instantly. You look around, but the familiar landmarks are gone; the trees now loom like ancient sentinels, their bark glistening with moisture. A path of glowing mushrooms appears, pulsing softly with an eerie blue light, leading deeper into the woods.

You follow it, heart pounding, as the storm intensifies. Lightning flashes, illuminating the forest in stark, fleeting moments—revealing a hidden clearing ahead, where a small, weathered cabin stands, its windows glowing with warm, inviting light. The wind howls, but the cabin seems untouched by the chaos, as if it exists in a pocket of calm.

You hurry toward it, hoping for shelter, and as you reach the door, you hear a soft voice from within: "You're safe now." The door creaks open, revealing a cozy interior with a crackling fire and a figure sitting in a chair, smiling gently. The storm rages outside, but inside, it feels like the heart of the world has paused, just for you.
