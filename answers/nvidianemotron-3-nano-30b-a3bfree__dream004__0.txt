When you open them again, the canvas erupts in that very sunset, spilling its amber glow across the entire surreal landscape. The once‑mundane hills now shimmer with liquid gold, the rivers turn to molten copper, and the sky becomes an endless canvas of **burning opal**, reflecting every hue you have ever imagined.

The blankness is gone. In its place is a **living, breathing dream**, a realm where colors and shapes continually morph, where each new discovery is a brushstroke of possibility.
