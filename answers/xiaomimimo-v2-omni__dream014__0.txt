As you walk together, the city’s chaos seems to soften. The lights glow brighter, the streets feel less crowded, and you realize you’re not just being led somewhere—you’re being welcomed. Before you part ways, one of them says, “You’re never really lost. You’re just on a different route for a while.”

You wake with a lingering sense of warmth, remembering their smiles—a quiet reminder that even in unfamiliar places, kindness can be the truest compass.
