…the sky darkens beneath a ceiling of rolling violet clouds. The trees begin to whisper, then bend, their leaves flashing silver in the rising wind.

Rain falls—not in drops, but in glowing threads of light.

You run deeper into the forest, following a narrow path that appears only when lightning flickers. At the end of it stands a small wooden door set into the trunk of an enormous oak. Warm golden light spills from beneath it.

When you open the door, you find not a room, but a staircase descending into the clouds.

Below, the storm is quiet.

You step down, and with each stair, the thunder becomes softer until it sounds like distant applause. At the bottom, there is a calm lake reflecting a sky full of stars. In the center floats a paper boat with your name written on its sail.

You climb inside.

The boat begins to drift across the mirror-like water, carrying you toward a sunrise that feels strangely familiar.
