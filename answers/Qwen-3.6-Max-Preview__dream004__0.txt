...a single brush that feels warm to the touch, humming with a low, resonant frequency.

As you reach for it, the air around you shimmers, dissolving into ribbons of liquid gold and deep indigo. The ground beneath your feet softens, turning into a plush carpet of moss that glows with bioluminescent pulses in time with your heartbeat.

You pick up the brush. It doesn’t have bristles; instead, its tip is made of swirling starlight. When you bring it close to the blank canvas, the white surface ripples like water disturbed by a stone. You realize that the canvas isn't just waiting for paint—it’s waiting for *intent*.

You hesitate, then make a gentle stroke.

Instead of color, a sound emerges—a clear, bell-like tone that hangs in the air. As the note sustains, shapes begin to bloom from the canvas: geometric flowers unfolding in slow motion, their petals made of translucent glass that refracts the shifting sky above. Each new shape adds another layer to the melody, creating a symphony of sight and sound.

Suddenly, you notice that the colors around you are responding to the music. The jagged mountains in the distance soften into rolling hills as the melody becomes more harmonious. The chaotic swirls of the sky organize themselves into intricate mandalas, spinning gently overhead.

You realize you aren't just painting on the canvas; you are painting the world itself. With every stroke, you reshape reality. A bold slash of crimson creates a river of fire that flows upward into the sky, while a delicate wash of blue cools the air, bringing forth a gentle rain of sparkling dust.

But then, a shadow falls across the canvas. Not a dark shadow, but an absence of light—a void that seems to pull at the edges of your creation. The shapes begin to unravel, the music distorting into dissonance. You feel a surge of panic, but also curiosity. Is this part of the dream? Or is something else trying to enter?

You raise the brush again, not to fight the void, but to understand it. You dip the starlight tip into the emptiness...

And wake up.
