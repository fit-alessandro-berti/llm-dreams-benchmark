You are in a maze filled with complex puzzles. Each puzzle requires intense concentration and logical thinking to solve, and as you move deeper into the maze, you begin to realize that every answer changes the shape of the corridors behind you.

Doors appear only when you stop looking for them. Numbers whisper from the walls. A chessboard stretches across a flooded room, its pieces moving whenever you blink. At the center of the maze, you find a final puzzle: a mirror showing you asleep, dreaming of a maze.

When you reach toward your reflection, it smiles first.
