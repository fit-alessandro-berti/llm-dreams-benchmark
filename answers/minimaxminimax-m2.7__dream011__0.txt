> **“If you could write the final chapter of your own story right now, what would you write, and would you let anyone else read it?”**

Your pen feels heavy, yet weightless. The tip glints with a silvery light, and as you press it to the paper, the words flow like liquid gold, forming sentences that seem to **rewrite themselves** the moment they appear.

> *“Answer with honesty, answer with imagination, answer with the part of you that has always dared to dream beyond the edge of the page.”*

You take a deep breath, feeling the surge of adrenaline mix with the calm of a lucid dream. The desk beneath you begins to **glow**, the wood turning into a translucent glass that reflects the swirling constellations above. You realize that the exam is not a test of right or wrong—it’s a **mirror for your inner compass**.

> **“In a world where your career is a living story, what story do you choose to tell, and who do you want to become while telling it?”**

The answer, you sense, is already woven into the pulse of your heartbeat, waiting for you to give it a name. As the paper folds itself into a delicate origami crane, you feel the weight of the future lift, just a little, and you know—whatever you write next will be the first line of a story that will unfold beyond the boundaries of this dream, into the waking world.
