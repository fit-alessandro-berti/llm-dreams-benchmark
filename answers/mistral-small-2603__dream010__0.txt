**The Dream Unfolds…**

The competition is held in a vast, ever-shifting arena—a surreal landscape where the rules bend like smoke. The first challenge is a **labyrinth of mirrors**, each reflection showing a different version of yourself: one holding a golden key, another drowning in ink, another standing atop a mountain of unanswered emails. You realize the mirrors aren’t just showing you—*they’re testing you*. To pass, you must choose which reflection to follow. Do you grab the key (opportunity?), drown in the ink (creative chaos?), or climb the mountain (ambition?)?

You choose the key.

The next challenge is a **silent auction** where the items aren’t gold or jewels, but **memories**. A stranger bids a childhood birthday party where you blew out candles shaped like question marks. Another bids the first time you failed at something and laughed it off. You hesitate—what’s the currency here? Then the auctioneer whispers: *"The prize isn’t the job. It’s the version of you who earned it."*

You bid your **fear of not being enough**.

The final challenge is a **door with no handle**, only a keyhole shaped like a question mark. A voice (familiar, yet not yours) says: *"The job is behind this door, but the lock only opens if you ask the right question."* You press your ear to the wood and hear the hum of a printer, the clatter of keyboards, the distant laughter of coworkers. It’s the sound of a workplace—but which one?

You whisper: *"What if I’m not ready?"*

The door creaks open.

Inside, the office is empty except for a single desk with a nameplate that reads: **"You (But Better)."** On the wall hangs a framed photo of your future self—older, wiser, but with the same eyes. A note says: *"The competition was never about the prize. It was about the person you became along the way."*

You wake up with the taste of possibility on your tongue.

---
**What happens next?** Do you:
- **Apply for the dream job anyway**, fueled by the dream’s confidence?
- **Ignore the job**, realizing the real prize was the self-discovery?
- **Dream again**, this time searching for the door’s key?
