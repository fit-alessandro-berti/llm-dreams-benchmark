You are feeling lost and alone in a bustling city. Suddenly, a group of kind strangers offers to help you find your way, and you join them gratefully. As you walk together, the chaotic streets soften into glowing paths of memory, the skyscrapers sway like gentle trees, and their faces flicker—shifting into old friends, childhood pets, and versions of your younger self. They lead you through a hidden door into an endless garden where lost pieces of your life wait under a sky of shooting stars. A warm sense of belonging washes over you... and then you wake, smiling, feeling a little less alone in the real world.
