You are walking through a peaceful forest when suddenly the weather changes dramatically. You feel a storm approaching and the trees begin to whisper in a language you almost understand.

Rain falls upward from the earth into the darkening sky.

Ahead, between two ancient oaks, a small golden door stands alone. Lightning flashes, and for a moment you see your own hand reaching from the other side, inviting you in.

You open it.

Instead of a room, there is an endless ocean beneath a violet moon. A paper boat floats at your feet with your name written on its sail. You step inside, and the boat carries you silently across the water.

Far away, something enormous rises from the sea—not frightening, but familiar. It is the forest itself, dreaming of you.

Then the thunder becomes the sound of someone softly calling your name, and you wake with the taste of rain on your lips.
