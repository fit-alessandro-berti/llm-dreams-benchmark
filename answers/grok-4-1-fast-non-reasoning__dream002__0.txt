You are walking through a peaceful forest when suddenly the weather changes dramatically. You feel a storm approaching and the sky darkens to an unnatural bruised purple. Thunder rumbles like the growl of some ancient beast awakening beneath the earth. You quicken your pace, branches whipping at your face as the first fat raindrops splatter down, cold and heavy.

Ahead, through the twisting trees, a crumbling stone archway emerges—ruins you've never noticed before, though you've wandered these woods a hundred times. Lightning cracks, illuminating carvings of eyes and serpents etched into the stone. A voice whispers your name on the wind, not in fear, but invitation. Heart pounding, you step through the archway...

...and the forest vanishes. You're standing on a vast, floating island adrift in a starry void, surrounded by glowing crystals that hum with forgotten songs. Below, the storm rages on, but up here, the air shimmers with possibility. A figure approaches from the mist—a shimmering version of yourself, smiling knowingly. "What do you seek?" it asks. The dream fractures into colors, waiting for your choice...
