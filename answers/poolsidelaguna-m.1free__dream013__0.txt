You choose the mirrors. As you step inside, the reflections begin to speak, offering truths you’ve avoided. The maze, you realize, isn’t just a test of logic—it’s a reckoning. Each solved puzzle unravels a thread of the labyrinth, and with it, a piece of the person you thought you were.
