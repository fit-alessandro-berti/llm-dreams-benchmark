
As the storm intensifies, leaves swirl around you and branches creak menacingly. Amidst this chaos, you encounter a mysterious figure standing atop a large, ancient tree. They seem to be made of shadows, shifting and changing form in the flickering light. Their voice echoes with an otherworldly calmness, saying, "To continue this dream, you must learn to control the elements." With that, a warm gust of wind sweeps through the forest, carrying with it tiny glowing orbs. These orbs float gently towards you, inviting you to take one. What do you choose to do?
