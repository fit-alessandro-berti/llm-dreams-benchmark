You start to run, but the streets twist and rearrange themselves like origami. The buildings have no names, only strange symbols. Panic bubbles in your chest as you realize your phone has no signal. Then, out of the corner of your eye, you see a shimmer—a cat made of soft, blue light, padding silently down an alley. It glances back, as if waiting, and you feel an inexplicable pull to follow. The cat leads you through a narrow archway you hadn't noticed before, and the city sounds fade into a gentle hum. Ahead, you see a door glowing faintly in the mist, a plaque beside it that reads, "Meeting Room: Now." You take a breath and reach for the handle.
