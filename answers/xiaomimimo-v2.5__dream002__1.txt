# 🌲⚡ A Dream in the Forest

You are walking through a peaceful forest when suddenly the weather changes dramatically. You feel a storm approaching, and the air grows thick with electricity.

**The sky shifts** — one moment golden sunlight filters through the canopy, the next, bruise-purple clouds swirl overhead like a slow-motion whirlpool. The temperature drops, and your breath becomes visible.

Then the wind rises.

Leaves spiral around you in a dance that feels almost *deliberate*, as if the forest itself is gathering its thoughts. A low rumble echoes — not thunder exactly, but something deeper, like the earth clearing its throat.

**You quicken your pace**, but the path ahead seems to shift and curve in impossible ways. The trees grow taller, their bark glowing faintly with bioluminescent patterns — spirals and waves that pulse like a heartbeat.

Rain begins to fall, but it falls *upward* — silver droplets rising from the mossy ground toward the darkening sky, defying everything you thought you knew.

Standing before you now is **an ancient wooden gate**, covered in moss and vines, slightly ajar. Warm light spills through the gap. A voice — gentle, like wind through autumn leaves — whispers:

> *"The storm is not coming for you. It's coming* ***with*** *you. Step through."*

Your hand reaches for the gate...

---

**What happens next? Do you step through, or do you stay in the forest?** 🌿✨
