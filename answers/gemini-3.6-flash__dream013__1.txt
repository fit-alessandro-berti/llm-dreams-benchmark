There is no final locked door here, no grand prize, and no wall blocking your way. There is only a vast, silent amphitheater beneath a slow-revolving sky of gears and constellations. In the middle stands a simple pedestal holding a pool of perfectly still, dark water. 

You approach it, preparing yourself for the ultimate, most difficult calculation of all. You lean over and look into the water, expecting a complex equation reflected back at you. Instead, you see a single, gentle prompt waiting on the surface of the ripple:

*"Now that you have solved everything else, remember how to wake up."*
