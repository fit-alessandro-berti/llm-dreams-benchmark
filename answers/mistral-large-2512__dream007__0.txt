Dreams about arguments with loved ones often reflect:
- **Unresolved conflict** (even if the argument in the dream is about something trivial, it might symbolize deeper tension).
- **Fear of abandonment** (the shifting environment could represent instability in the relationship).
- **Self-doubt** (the mirrors showing "other versions" of your friend might symbolize your own insecurities about how you’re perceived).
- **A need for closure** (the dream forcing you to "wake up" could be your subconscious urging you to address the issue in real life).
