# ⛵ The Storm — A Dream

*I take a deep breath, salt spray stinging my face as waves crash against the hull.*

"We didn't sail this far to be swallowed by the sea!" *I shout over the howling wind, gripping the wheel with white knuckles.* "Hard to starboard — NOW! Let the wave roll beneath us!"

*The crew scrambles. Ropes tighten. Sails snap and tear, but we hold course.*

*I glance at the horizon. Through the chaos, there — a sliver of darkness where the clouds break apart. Stars. Faint, but real.*

**"THERE! See that gap in the sky? That's our heading. That's where we ride this out."**

*My first mate hesitates. "Captain, the current pulls us south—"*

**"Then we fight it."** *I grab the compass, spin it toward the clearing sky.* **"Every one of you chose to board this ship. That means we sail *together*, or we don't sail at all. NOW PULL!"**

*The crew roars back — fear still in their eyes, but something else now too. Trust.*

*The ship lurches. Another wave towers above us, dark as midnight, and for a moment everything goes silent—*

*Then we crest it.*

*The storm still rages behind us, but ahead the sea grows calmer. Moonlight spills across the water like a path laid just for us.*

*I loosen my grip on the wheel. My hands are shaking.*

**"Hold steady, crew. We're not home yet... but we will be."**

---

*And then I wake up.* 🌊✨
