You keep searching through the crowd and finally notice your person at the far end of the room, lit by a rectangle of pale moonlight from a window that wasn’t there before. Just as you begin to reach them, the floor gently tilts beneath you, and everyone in the room starts to sway like trees in a wind no one else can feel.

Then you realize the gathering was never a party at all, but a waiting room for a journey you forgot you were taking. Your person turns, sees you, and smiles with relief, as if they had been searching for you too.

And then you wake up, with the feeling that something important almost made sense.
