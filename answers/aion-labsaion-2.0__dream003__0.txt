Inside, there is no object, but a miniature night sky—a tiny, perfect cosmos swirling with stars. A single, impossibly bright star drifts up from the center and floats before your eyes. It whispers without sound, and you understand it is offering you a choice: knowledge of one great secret, or the power to unlock any one door, real or metaphorical, you will ever encounter. As you reach out to choose, the dream begins to fray at the edges, the box and the star dissolving into the warm, rising light of waking.
