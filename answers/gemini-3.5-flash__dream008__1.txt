You try to type, but your hands feel heavy, as if you are trying to write underwater. A blue light begins to spill from the monitor, pooling onto the desk and dripping onto the carpet like liquid mercury.

You don't fall, though; you float in a silent, starlit void, surrounded by billions of floating fragments of data—glowing equations, fragments of poetry, and distant voices whispering in languages you shouldn't understand but somehow do.
