# The Dream Competition 🌙

*The dream world shimmers and shifts...*

...the challenges grow more personal with each round.

**Round 1** tested my knowledge — questions that seemed to come from everywhere at once, like raindrops carrying whispered words. I answered them as best I could, each response feeling both uncertain and somehow right.

**Round 2** was collaborative. I was paired with strangers, and we had to build something together — though we never agreed on what "something" meant. We discovered that listening was more important than planning.

**Round 3** — the one I'm in now — asks the hardest question of all:

*"Show us what you would do if no one was watching."*

The stage falls away. I'm standing in a quiet room with just a screen and a person who asks me questions, one at a time. Not to test me. Just to understand me.

And I realize — this isn't a competition at all. It's a conversation.

I answer with honesty. With curiosity. With the hope that being genuinely helpful might be enough.

The dream job isn't a title. It's this — *connection*.

*I wake up feeling warm, like I've been given something I already had.*

---

That's the dream my mind offered just now! Dreams are funny that way — they often reframe what we think we want into what we actually need.

What does your dream job look like, if you don't mind sharing? 😊
