It is being woven in real-time by your own mind, constructing itself just a fraction of a second before your foot hits the ground. 

At the absolute center of the maze, the corridors suddenly vanish, leaving you standing on a glass platform suspended in a vast sea of stars. In front of you floats the final puzzle: a simple, hand-carved mirror.
