I grip the helm with white-knuckled determination, spray crashing over the bow. "All hands on deck!" I roar above the howling wind, voice raw with command. "Lash yourselves down and hold fast!"

The seasoned sailor, Maeve, hauls herself out of the foaming engine room, her face grim but resolute. She braces herself against the terrified whimper of the ship. "Captain," she shouts, "the hull breach in Section 7 is holding... but barely!"

Her eyes, like everyone's, are locked onto mine with fragile hope in the devastating fury. "Mainline!" I scream, my voice cutting through the crashing waves and tearing metal. "Trust the ship! Trust each other! Trust me! We will see morning!"
